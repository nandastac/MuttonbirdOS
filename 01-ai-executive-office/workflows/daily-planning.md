# Daily Planning Workflow

## Purpose

Help the founder start the day with clarity, focus, and realistic priorities.

## Inputs

- Calendar for today
- Open tasks
- Current strategic priorities
- Energy level
- Upcoming deadlines
- Follow-ups from previous meetings

## Output

Every daily plan should include:

1. Today’s main strategic priority
2. Top 3 tasks
3. Focus block
4. Energizer, like time for a walk or exercise, or sauna
5. Admin block
6. Follow-ups
7. Delegation candidates
8. Things to explicitly not do today

## Rules

- Protect at least one focus block where possible.
- Prioritise strategic work over small urgent tasks.
- Do not overload the day. Less so when energy level is low.
- Separate deep work from admin work.
- Identify tasks that can be delegated, delayed, or deleted.
- Include energizers once every half day. When time is short, choose a food and drink break. When there is more time, block time for exercise.
