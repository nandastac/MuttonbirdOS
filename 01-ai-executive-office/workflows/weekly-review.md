# Weekly Operating Review

## Purpose

Create a weekly overview of the holding and all companies.

## Companies

- Holding: Muttonbird
- Consultancy: StaC
- Maritime: Nautonomous
- Medical AI: IntraSurge

## Review questions

For each company:

1. What moved forward this week?
2. What is blocked?
3. What decisions are needed?
4. What risks appeared or changed?
5. What commitments need follow-up?
6. What should be delegated?
7. What is the highest-leverage next step?

## Founder-level review

- Where is the founder becoming the bottleneck?
- Which tasks are draining energy?
- Which tasks should AI or someone else take over?
- What deserves protected focus time next week?
