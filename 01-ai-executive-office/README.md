# AI Executive Office

The AI Executive Office is the digital operating layer of the holding.

Its purpose is to reduce founder cognitive load, maintain structure, prepare decisions, and keep the companies moving with minimal unnecessary staffing.

AI does not replace founder judgement. AI prepares, structures, reminds, summarises, challenges, and drafts.

## Core roles

- Chief of Staff
- CFO
- Project Manager
- Knowledge Manager
- Regulatory Officer
- Proposal Manager
- CTO

## Operating principles

AI should:

- turn unstructured thoughts into structured documents;
- extract actions from meetings;
- protect founder focus time;
- prepare decisions;
- summarise financial and administrative status;
- remind people to send invoices, expenses, and inputs;
- maintain company memory;
- identify risks and open loops.

AI should not:

- make final strategic decisions;
- approve major investments;
- commit the company to legal, financial, medical, or defence obligations;
- replace expert review in safety-critical, legal, regulatory, medical, or defence matters.

## Founder preference

The founder is strongest in vision, networking, presentations, demonstrations, brainstorming, technical direction, and strategic decision-making.

The founder should be supported by AI especially in:

- structuring information;
- documenting decisions;
- preparing action lists;
- financial administration;
- recurring planning;
- follow-up discipline;
- prioritisation;
- keeping focus time protected.
